ke = ln(Pico / Vale) / (T2 – T1)

Pico Real = Pico Medido / e–ke·(T1 – Tinf)

Vale Real = Vale Medido · e–ke·(Tau – T2)

AUCinf = (Pico Real + Vale Real) / 2 · Tinf

AUCelim = (Pico Real – Vale Real) / ke

AUC24 = (AUCinf + AUCelim) · (24 / Tau)

VD = Dose · (1 – e–ke·Tinf) / (Tinf · ke · (Pico Real – Vale Real · e–ke·Tinf))

CL = VD · ke

Pico Previsto = (Nova Dose / (CL · Novo Tinf)) · (1 – e–ke·Novo Tinf) / (1 – e–ke·Novo Tau)

Vale Previsto = Pico Previsto · e–ke·(Novo Tau – Novo Tinf)
